＃御巫
Oh, is something the matter?

No, I was just heading back to my room... How about you, Doctor?
＃御巫
Well... I was just checking on Maya-san since she hasn't been feeling particularly well.

＃主人公
Is she going to be alright?
＃御巫
It might just be her nerves acting up before the ceremony, but... honestly, I wish she'd give me a break.
＃主人公
That sounds rough.

＃御巫
Well, I'm the attending physician, so I suppose I have no choice.

＃御巫
Anyway, I'll be off. I still have things to take care of.
＃主人公
Oh, right. ...Take care of yourself.
